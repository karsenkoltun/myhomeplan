## How My Home Plan Fixes This

### For Homeowners

1. **Go to myhomeplan.ca**
2. **Tell us about your home** - size, lot, location, heating type
3. **Pick your services** - lawn care, snow removal, cleaning, HVAC, pest control, handyman, whatever you need
4. **See your monthly price instantly** - a live receipt builds on screen as you select services
5. **Subscribe** - choose monthly, quarterly (5% off), or annual (15% off)
6. **Schedule through your dashboard** - book any service, anytime throughout the year
7. **We send a vetted contractor** - licensed, insured, background-checked
8. **Rate the service** - your feedback keeps quality high

**That's it.** One bill. One dashboard. One contact for everything.

### For Contractors

1. **Apply at myhomeplan.ca/contractors**
2. **Get vetted** - we verify your license, insurance, and references
3. **Get listed** - homeowners in your area can see your profile
4. **Receive guaranteed work** - no more chasing leads. Jobs come to you.
5. **Get paid fast** - within 7 days, guaranteed. No more chasing invoices.
6. **Build your reputation** - great reviews = more jobs

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: What if I need a service that's not on my plan?**
A: You can add any service a la carte through your dashboard. You'll get our network pricing (typically 10-15% below market rate).

**Q: Can I change my plan?**
A: Yes. Upgrade anytime. Downgrades take effect at your next billing cycle.

**Q: What if a contractor does bad work?**
A: Rate them honestly. We'll send a replacement at no cost. Contractors with consistently low ratings are removed from our network.

**Q: Is there a contract?**
A: Monthly plans are month-to-month. Cancel anytime. Quarterly and annual plans are prepaid but refundable on a prorated basis.

**Q: How do you vet contractors?**
A: Business license verification, proof of insurance ($2M liability minimum), WorkSafe BC coverage, background check, reference check, and a probation period with quality monitoring.

**Q: Do I pick my contractor?**
A: You can browse available contractors in your area and request specific ones. Otherwise, we match you with the highest-rated available pro.
